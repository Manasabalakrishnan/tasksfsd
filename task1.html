Once upon a time, a kind girl named Cinderella lived with her cruel stepmother and stepsisters who forced her to do all the housework, earning her the name "Cinderella.". When an invitation arrived for a royal ball, Cinderella's stepmother and stepsisters gleefully prepared to attend, forbidding Cinderella from going. Devastated, Cinderella wished to attend the ball, and just then, her Fairy Godmother appeared, transforming her rags into a beautiful gown, a carriage, and glass slippers. At the ball, Cinderella danced with the Prince, captivating him with her grace and beauty, but she had to rush home at midnight, leaving behind one of her glass slippers. The Prince, determined to find the mysterious girl, searched the kingdom, using the glass slipper to identify her. When the slipper perfectly fit Cinderella's foot, the Prince realized she was the girl from the ball. Despite her stepmother and stepsisters' attempts to deceive him, Cinderella and the Prince married and lived happily ever after
